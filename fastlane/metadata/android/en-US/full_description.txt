Appiary turns your hive records into clear, prioritized next steps. Open it and see what to do today, what's due soon, which colonies are stable, and — crucially — why each action is suggested.

Every recommendation comes from a deterministic, explainable rules engine, not an AI model. There is no account, no cloud, and no network requirement: your data stays on your device.

Features:

* Today — "Do now / Due soon / Watchlist / Healthy" priorities, each card with a plain-language explanation of the inputs behind it
* Apiaries & hives — status, next action, search, a unified per-hive timeline, photos and quick logging
* Fast logging — inspections, mite checks, treatments, feeding, harvests and notes in seconds
* Insights — honey-yield, varroa-load (with treatment thresholds) and inspection-activity charts
* Colony lifecycle — record splits, swarm captures and merges, with parent/daughter lineage
* Hive weight — log scale readings over time
* Seasonal & climate guidance — a per-apiary season model derived offline from your coordinates, refined with open weather data when online
* Map — an offline OpenStreetMap of your apiary locations
* Home-screen widget — today's urgent tasks at a glance
* Reminders, full JSON/CSV backup and optional NFC hive tags
* German "Bestandsbuch" veterinary-medicine record-keeping (EU 2019/6)

Privacy: the only data ever sent off your device is apiary coordinates, and only to the keyless Open-Meteo weather service to refine the local season estimate. Everything else works fully offline.

Fully available in English and German.
